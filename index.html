⚔ HOW TO PLAY

Learn the way of the gladiator strategist

Chess basics: Each player controls 16 pieces. White moves first. The goal is to checkmate — trap the enemy king with no escape.

Special moves:
• Castling — King and rook swap if neither has moved and the path is clear.
• En Passant — A pawn can capture a pawn that just moved two squares past it.
• Promotion — A pawn reaching the far rank becomes a queen.

Assist Mode shows green and yellow arrows suggesting your best moves.

🏛 THE ARENA

Blood, bronze, and strategy

The Concept

Arena Chess: Gladiator War reimagines chess as a cinematic gladiatorial spectacle inside a grand Roman colosseum. Every piece is a bronze warrior. Every move is a tactical command. Every capture is a duel.

The Pieces

♔ King — The Roman Emperor. His fall ends the war.
♕ Queen — The Champion Gladiator. Most powerful on the sand.
♖ Rook — The Roman Fortress Tower. Controls the flanks.
♗ Bishop — The Roman Strategist. Commands the diagonals.
♘ Knight — The Cavalry Warrior. Leaps over the battlefield.
♙ Pawn — The Roman Foot Soldier. The heart of any army.

The 3D Environment

Rendered in real-time with Three.js. Dynamic torch lighting, camera shake, particle sparks, and cinematic capture animations make every duel feel alive. The colosseum walls, sand floor, and crowd silhouettes immerse you in ancient Rome.

The AI

The bot uses Negamax with Alpha-Beta pruning — the same algorithm family used in professional chess engines. It evaluates material balance and piece positioning to choose intelligent moves quickly.
